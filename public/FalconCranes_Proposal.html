Our Approach

How we build it.
Four principles we hold to.

Every decision in this platform flows from these four rules. They exist because AI systems fail quietly, and we would rather you trust it than be surprised by it.

Human in the loop at every gate
AI drafts, people approve. Every output (proposal, job plan, contract, onboarding pack) is reviewed and confirmed by a person before it goes anywhere. The AI does the legwork. the team makes the calls.
Every output cites its source
Every number, every recommendation, every clause traces back to the specific file or spreadsheet it came from. "Crane A recommended, based on Job #231 (similar radius, same anchor type)" is the standard. If you can't verify it, we didn't build it right.
Simple, role-based UX
The right people see the right things. No one looks at a screen full of data that isn't theirs. The interface is designed for the team as it is today, not for a team that has already adopted Gantt charts and project management software.
Every correction trains the next result
When the team edits a proposal, that edit is a signal. When HR rejects a contract and changes the pay rate, that correction is logged. The system gets more accurate every week it runs on real Falcon Cranes data, not on generic training.
WHAT TO EXPECT: HONEST NUMBERS
The system launches at ~70% accuracy, the industry standard for a new AI working on real operational data. It is already faster and more consistent than manual. Over the first 6–12 months, as corrections feed back and the data layer matures, accuracy climbs toward 85–90%+. We build in the feedback loop from day one so this happens automatically, not through another engagement.
