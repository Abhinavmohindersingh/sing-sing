Timeline — Why This Order
Website first — the configurator needs a home
The 3D configurator lives inside the TES website. Before we can build it, the design system, URL structure, product page templates, and hosting environment need to exist. Phase 1 is the foundation everything else runs on.
Configurator second — now it has context
By Week 7 the site is live and indexed. The configurator builds on top of the existing product pages and design language — consistent, not bolted on. Buyers who land on a product page can go straight into the configurator without a jarring transition.
Phase 3 is the AI — and it's the whole point
The AI companion qualifies every buyer before they call — which crane for which job, at which site. It runs live spec comparisons, routes hot leads straight to AJ, and replaces three to five hours of early-stage sales calls per week. The site and configurator are the foundation. The AI is the payoff.
Not a RAG pipeline — Claude with guardrails
This is not a search engine over thousands of documents. It's Claude (Anthropic's AI) wired to TES's product specs, pricing, and a structured qualification flow. BQ's full crane catalogue fits comfortably in a single context window — no vector database, no embedding pipeline, no infrastructure to maintain. When specs change, you update a file. That's why Phase 3 is two weeks, not three months.
8 weeks total. Phases overlap.
Phase 2 starts while Phase 1 is still in QA. Phase 3 AI build starts while the configurator is in testing. You're not waiting for one thing to finish before the next begins — the team runs in parallel so the full handover is at Week 8.
