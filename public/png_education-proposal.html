The Reality

A child walks to school this morning.
There is no teacher.

86%
Too Far Away
86% of PNG's people live in rural or highland communities. Before any other factor applies, distance has already decided what this child's education will look like.
10K
No One to Teach
10,000 teacher positions sit empty. In the schools that have one teacher — that one person covers 200+ students, every subject, every grade, every day.
80%
No Power. No Signal.
80% of schools have no reliable power. 73% have no internet. Any solution that needs a connection leaves most of this country behind before it starts.
72% of 10-year-olds in PNG cannot read at grade level. This is not a statistic about the future. It is happening right now — in the years when the brain learns fastest and most permanently.
